## Grammar Focus: Zero Conditional  
Structure: `If + Present Simple, Present Simple`

### Examples:
- *If a horse throws off a rider, the rider gets back on immediately.*  
- *If you tighten the girth properly, the saddle stays in place.*  
- *If you are loud, the horse gets scared.*
